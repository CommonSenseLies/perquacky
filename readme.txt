Perquackey is a game for 1 or more players. Open one copy of this tab for each player. Each player takes a turn in order.

On your turn, you have 3 minutes to make as many words as you can (up to 5 of each length).

Words must be:
  - At least 3 letters
  - No proper nouns (BOSTON, XEROX, HENRY)
  - For S plurals: you can use DOG or DOGS, but not both

After the round ends, you will receive a score based on how many words of each length you spelled. Additionally, you get bonuses for finishing entire groups of 5 words:
  +300 points for having all 3-letter and 4-letter words
  +500 points for having all 4-letter and 5-letter words
  +600 points for having all 5-letter and 6-letter words
  +1200 points for having all 6-letter and 7-letter words
  +1850 points for having all 7-letter and 8-letter words

Once you reach 2000 points, you are "vulnerable".
  - You can no longer form 3-letter words.
  - If you score under 500 in a round, you receive in penalty (of -500 points).
  - You get three extra red dice each round.

The first player to 5000 points wins.
